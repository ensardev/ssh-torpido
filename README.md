## What is this?

**torpido** is the classic naval strategy game, playable entirely over SSH:

- ⚡ **Zero friction** — no download, no account. Your SSH key is your identity.
- ⚔️ **1v1 battles** — quick match, or create a room and send the invite code to a friend.
- 🤖 **Bots always ready** — three difficulty tiers waiting in the lobby, 24/7.
- 🏠 **Rooms & lobby** — public rooms, password-protected rooms, spectating (soon).
- 📊 **Persistent stats** — your wins, streaks and ELO follow your SSH key.
